2. Personal Information We Collect

We only collect personal information that is necessary to respond to your enquiry or provide our services (the principle of minimality — POPIA Section 10).

Information you provide directly

When you submit the contact form on this website, we collect:

  • Full name — to address you correctly
  • Email address — to respond to your enquiry
  • Project subject — to understand the nature of your request
  • Message content — the details of your enquiry

Information collected automatically

When you visit this website, our hosting provider (Cloudflare) may automatically collect:

  • IP address — for security and DDoS protection purposes
  • Browser type and version — for compatibility purposes
  • Date and time of visit — for server logs

This information is collected by Cloudflare and governed by Cloudflare's Privacy Policy. We do not use third-party analytics tools (e.g. Google Analytics) and we do not place tracking cookies.

3. Purpose of Processing

We process your personal information only for the following specific, legitimate purposes (POPIA Section 13):

  • To respond to your project enquiry or question
  • To assess whether we can assist with your requirements
  • To send you an automated acknowledgement that your enquiry was received
  • To follow up on potential or existing client engagements

We will not use your personal information for any other purpose without obtaining your consent first.

4. Lawful Basis for Processing

We process your personal information on the following lawful grounds (POPIA Section 11):

  • Consent — by submitting the contact form, you consent to us processing your information to respond to your enquiry.
  • Legitimate interest — we have a legitimate interest in communicating with prospective and existing clients to provide our services.
  • Contractual necessity — where we have an existing service agreement with you, processing is necessary to perform our obligations.

7. Retention of Personal Information

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected (POPIA Section 14):

  • Contact form enquiries — retained for up to 3 years from the date of last contact, or for the duration of any resulting business relationship, whichever is longer.
  • Client project records — retained for 5 years after project completion, as required for accounting and legal purposes.
  • Email communications — retained for as long as the business relationship exists plus 3 years.

After the applicable retention period, personal information is securely deleted or anonymised.

8. Security of Your Information

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information from loss, misuse, unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction (POPIA Section 19). These measures include:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmitted to and from this website (TLS 1.2 / 1.3)
  • Secure storage of API keys and secrets in Cloudflare's encrypted environment variables (not in source code)
  • Access to personal information restricted to Jan-Louis Reynders only
  • Third-party operators selected based on their security standards and contractual commitments

In the event of a data breach that is likely to result in a risk to your rights, we will notify the Information Regulator and affected data subjects as required by POPIA Section 22.

9. Your Rights as a Data Subject

Under POPIA (Sections 5, 23–25), you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right of access — you may request confirmation of whether we hold your personal information and request a copy.
  • Right to correction — you may request that inaccurate, incomplete, or outdated information be corrected or updated.
  • Right to deletion — you may request that your personal information be deleted where we no longer have a lawful basis or legitimate reason to retain it.
  • Right to object — you may object to the processing of your personal information on reasonable grounds.
  • Right to withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.
  • Right not to be subject to automated decision-making — we do not make legally significant automated decisions about you.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at jan-louis@jlrdev.co.za. We will respond within a reasonable time and no later than 30 days.
